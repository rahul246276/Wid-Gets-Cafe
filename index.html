Everything you need to know before enrolling.

What is the minimum qualification required?
No specific qualification is required. Even a 10th pass student can enroll. All you need is passion for technology and a willingness to learn.
Do you provide placement after course completion?
Yes! We have a dedicated placement cell with tie-ups with 50+ repair centers and mobile companies in Delhi NCR. We also guide students in setting up their own repair shops.
Is the certificate recognized by companies?
Our certificates are industry-recognized and accepted by leading mobile service centers, Apple Authorized Service Providers, Samsung Service Centers, and other top companies.

Course fees vary by program. We offer flexible EMI options to make education accessible. Contact us or visit the institute for the latest fee structure and offers.
