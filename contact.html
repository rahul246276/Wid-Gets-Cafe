FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our courses and admission process.

Do I need prior technical knowledge to enroll?
Not at all! We start from basic electronics fundamentals and gradually advance to complex topics. Many of our successful students had zero technical background before joining.
What documents are required for admission?
You'll need: ID proof (Aadhar/PAN), 2 passport photos, and the admission form. We provide the form at our institute during enrollment.
Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes! We have a dedicated placement cell with tie-ups with 50+ service centers. We also help students who want to start their own repair business.
Are EMI options available for course fees?
Absolutely! We offer flexible EMI options to make education accessible. You can pay in easy monthly installments with zero interest.
