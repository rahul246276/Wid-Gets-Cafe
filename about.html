About Wid Gets Cafe

Empowering young technicians since 2009 with world-class mobile repairing education right in the heart of Delhi.

Our Story

Delhi's Most Trusted
Repair Training Institute

Founded in 2009, Wid Gets Cafe has grown from a small training center to Delhi's most recognized mobile repairing institute. Located in the heart of Uttam Nagar, we've trained over 5,000 students who are now running successful careers and businesses across India.

Our philosophy is simple — learn by doing. Every student who walks through our doors gets hands-on experience with real devices, professional tools, and expert guidance that prepares them for the real world from day one.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯
Our Mission

To provide world-class, affordable, and practical mobile repairing education that empowers students from all backgrounds to build successful, sustainable careers in the growing smartphone repair industry. We are committed to hands-on learning, expert mentorship, and continuous skill development.

🔭
Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and recognized mobile technology training institution — a place where every student leaves with not just a certificate, but the confidence, skills, and business mindset to lead the future of the mobile repair industry across the nation.

Facilities

World-Class Infrastructure

Our lab is equipped with the most advanced tools used in top professional repair centers.

🔬
Professional BGA Lab
Our BGA reballing lab features professional-grade BGA machines, rework stations, and precision soldering equipment for advanced chip-level training.
🖥️
Software Flashing Lab
Dedicated software section with JTAG boxes, Octoplus, UFI, and all major flash tools for complete mobile software training.
⚡
Power & Testing Lab
DC power supplies, digital oscilloscopes, component testers, and multimeters for fault diagnosis and power-level training.
🔧
Hardware Workshop
Fully equipped hardware workshop with hot air rework stations, soldering irons, magnification tools, and all precision instruments.
📚
Digital Learning Room
Smart classroom with projectors and digital content for theory sessions, circuit diagram studies, and video demonstrations.
🏢
Student Lounge
Comfortable lounge area for students to relax, network, and collaborate — because great technicians are built outside the lab too.
