About Wid Gets Cafe

Empowering young technicians since 2009 — Delhi's most trusted mobile repairing training institute in the heart of Uttam Nagar.

Our Story

Delhi's Most Trusted
Repair Training Institute

Founded in 2009, Wid Gets Cafe has grown from a small training center into Delhi's most recognized mobile repairing institute. Located in the heart of Uttam Nagar, we have trained over 5,000 students who are now running successful careers and businesses across India.

Our philosophy is simple — learn by doing. Every student who walks through our doors gets hands-on experience with real devices, professional tools, and expert guidance that prepares them for the real world from day one.

We believe that a skill in your hands is worth more than a degree on paper. That's why our training is purely practical, industry-aligned, and designed to get you job-ready or business-ready in the shortest possible time.

Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class, affordable, and practical mobile repairing education that empowers students from all backgrounds to build successful, sustainable careers in the growing smartphone repair industry. We are committed to hands-on learning, expert mentorship, and continuous skill development.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and recognized mobile technology training institution — a place where every student leaves with not just a certificate, but with confidence, skills, and a business mindset to lead the future of the mobile repair industry across the nation.
